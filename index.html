What makes concerts at Fremantle Arts Centre so special?

Nestled among lush gardens and surrounded by heritage buildings in the heart of the City of Fremantle, the concert space at The Fremantle Arts Centre provides a tranquil yet vibrant and quintessetially Fremantle atmosphere. There's no where else like it in Western Australia.

This much loved heritage building houses unique art exhibitions by day, but transforms into the ultimate outdoor live music venue at night.

Party under the stars as the Fremantle Doctor keeps you cool. Then head out into the night and experience the best of Fremantle night life after the show.
